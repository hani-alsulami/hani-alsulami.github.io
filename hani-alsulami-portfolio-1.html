About

I lead the SOC inside the corporate cybersecurity function of a Saudi digital bank — the team responsible for detecting, triaging, and responding to threats around the clock. My background is in digital forensics and incident response, and I bring that same evidence-first discipline to how the team is built, staffed, and measured.

Day to day that means owning detection strategy, incident command during major events, playbook and use-case development, and translating what the SOC sees into decisions the wider business can act on.

Outside of work, I publish breakdowns of emerging CVEs and forensic techniques for the wider security community — the kind of notes I wish existed when I was coming up through this field.
