About

I work in digital forensics and incident response, currently operating inside the corporate cybersecurity function of a Saudi digital bank. My day-to-day sits at the intersection of SOC operations and deep-dive forensics — triaging alerts, then following the artifacts far enough to explain exactly what happened, how, and what to fix.

Outside of my role, I publish breakdowns of emerging CVEs, malware persistence techniques, and forensic artifact analysis for the wider security community — the kind of notes I wish existed when I was learning this.

I care about evidence over assumption. Every case here started as a hunch and ended as a documented, defensible finding.
